# _NDIS_RW_LOCK_REFCOUNT structure


## -description


The <b>NDIS_RW_LOCK_REFCOUNT</b> union defines attributes of a read/write lock. This struct is opaque to NDIS
   drivers.
   
## -struct-fields

### -field RefCount

This struct is opaque to NDIS drivers.

### -field cacheLine

This struct is opaque to NDIS drivers.
