## -description

The **ACX_PIN_CALLBACKS_INIT** function initializes an [ACX_PIN_CALLBACKS](ns-acxpin-acx_pin_callbacks.md) structure for use.

## -parameters

### -param PinCallbacks [out]

A pointer to the **ACX_PIN_CALLBACKS** structure to initialize.
